YIIFF Travelling

The term “TRAVELLING YIIFF” refers to a program where YIIF travels inside Nepal screening movies, free of cost for the educational and awareness purpose for the general public. This program intends to make the general public aware of cinema culture in and around the globe encouraging the young independent voices and stories.

NOTE: This is optional for the makers, only if they (film maker) wanted to participate in this program.

If agreed follow the following
link: http://www.yiiff.org/travelingyiiff

ACCEPTANCE OF RULES: Submitting a signed entry form constitutes full acceptance of the YIIFF rules outlined herein and on the entry form. YIIFF reserves the right to decide all questions not specifically addressed.
