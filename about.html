Introduction

Yala International Independent Film festival is the initiative of Nepal Independent Films, an organization that represents independent film makers in Nepal. At a time when the local film industry is still recuperating from the negative effects the civil war, this festival seeks to provide independent Nepalese film makers with a platform to showcase their films utilizing an ancient city known for its art and architecture as an inspirational backdrop. In addition to giving a voice to under-represented film makers, the festival seeks to provide visual advocacy for the betterment of society, showcase independent films from the international arena and become a venue for film makers from around the world to engage in artistic discussions and interactions.

Mission Statement

To inspire and bring to the forefront the unheard independent voices of aspiring film makers around the globe.

Vision

Our ultimate goal is to raise the profile of this festival internationally and make it a prestigious event in international film circles and benefit those unheard voices and independent stories. Our hopes are that such a gain in stature would shine a much needed spot light on the local film industry, inspire local talents and provide a prestigious platform for independent film makers from around the world.
