Official Rules and Regulations for the Yala International Independent Film Festival (YIIFF).

Eligibility

  • Productions produced after January 2017 is eligible.
  • Entries must be postmarked by the deadline date.
  • Entrant confirms and warrants required legal authority to submit the entry into the Festival and to use all music, images and content in the entry.
  • Entrant will allow usage of film for festival screening regardless of any distribution deals made.
  • Entrant will allow usage of clips from the entry and all related publicity material submitted for promotional use on television, radio, in print, web and at live Festival events.
  • Upon notification of acceptance, trailers may be sent via email links to YouTube. This is a great marketing for getting an audience excited about your film.
  • All Decisions by judges, programmers and organizers will be final and no refund of entry fee will be permitted.
  • Filmmakers will pay all transportation costs of shipping exhibition Blu-Rays / DVDs to festival.
  • Screening DVD"s must be labeled with the work’s title, running time, director(s) name(s), contact number, Production company(if), email and WAB Tracking number.
  • DVD SCREENERS, EXHIBITION BLU-RAY"S and Flash Drives WILL NOT BE RETURNED.

Submission Format

  • Films must be submitted via secure online screener.
  • Submission Eligibility
    -All submissions must have been completed after January 1, 2017.
  • Premiere Status
    - NEPAL Premiere status is preferred.
  • Selection of Films
    - YALA International Independent Film Festival will complete the selection process by December 15, 2018 and will contact all submitting parties by email with programming status at that time. Festival will invite accepted film’s director/Producer to attend the festival. Festival will provide food and accommodation during Festival.

  • The festival aspires to provide the best quality projection possible but will not be held liable for any failure in the technical quality of the projection, nor will fees be refunded in any such case.

Additional Submission Rules

  • Works in progress will automatically be disqualified.
  • No press materials will be accepted until films have been announced.
  • If your film is accepted, you will receive a notification email from the festival. Your acceptance will be conditional on meeting promotional/press kit, informational and film print submission deadlines.
